London: Designed by Time 倫敦：時光雕琢之城

The city's genius isn't a single masterplan, but a resilient, layered evolution. A palimpsest written over two millennia. 這座城市的非凡之處並非源於單一的宏偉藍圖，而是一場堅韌、層疊的演進。一部書寫了兩千年的重寫本。

The Circulatory System 循環系統

The Underground: Shaping the Metropolis 地鐵：塑造大都會

Opened in 1863, the Tube didn't just move people; it actively designed modern London by driving suburban growth along its arteries. 地鐵於1863年開通，它不僅僅是運送乘客，更通過沿其動脈推動郊區發展，積極地設計了現代倫敦。

Managing the Surface 地面交通管理

Above ground, policy shifted from accommodating cars to managing demand, prioritizing people and public transport. 在地面上，政策從容納汽車轉向管理需求，優先考慮行人和公共交通。

A Tale of Two Greens 雙綠傳奇

Lungs Within, Walls Without 城內之肺，城外之牆

London's greenness comes from a dual system: the cherished Royal Parks providing internal oases, and the vast Green Belt acting as a powerful, if contested, boundary. 倫敦的綠意來自一個雙重系統：珍貴的皇家公園提供內部綠洲，而廣闊的綠帶則作為一道強大但備受爭議的邊界。

The Architectural Tapestry 建築織錦

A Dialogue of Styles 風格的對話

Each era left its mark, creating a cityscape that is a living museum of architectural history, unified by a consistent palette of high-quality materials. 每個時代都留下了自己的印記，創造出一個活生生的建築史博物館般的城市景觀，並由一系列高品質的材料統一風格。

Georgian (1714-1830)

喬治亞時期 (1714-1830)

Elegant order, symmetry, and understated grandeur in brick townhouses and garden squares.

典雅的秩序、對稱性和低調的宏偉，體現在磚砌聯排別墅和花園廣場中。

Victorian (1837-1901)

維多利亞時期 (1837-1901)

Exuberant and eclectic, with ornate detailing, patterned brickwork, and bay windows.

繁複多樣，裝飾華麗，有圖案的磚砌和凸窗。

Modern & Brutalist (Post-WWII)

現代與粗獷主義 (二戰後)

Austere aesthetics, repetition of form, and widespread use of raw concrete for mass housing.

簡樸的美學，形式的重複，以及為大眾住宅廣泛使用原始混凝土。

Contemporary High-Tech (21st C)

當代高科技 (21世紀)

Glass and steel skyscrapers shaped by protected view corridors, blending innovation with heritage.

由受保護的視線走廊塑造的玻璃和鋼結構摩天大樓，將創新與遺產融為一體。

Designing for People 為人而設計

Legible London: The Power of Wayfinding 清晰倫敦：路標的力量

A unified, user-centered system replaced 32 confusing signage types to make the city walkable and intuitive, reducing the cognitive load of navigation. 一個統一的、以用戶為中心的系統取代了32種令人困惑的標誌類型，使城市變得適合步行且直觀，減輕了導航的認知負擔。

Guiding the Future: "Good Growth" 引領未來：「優質增長」

The modern London Plan guides all development with a philosophy of "Good Growth"—growth that is socially inclusive, environmentally sustainable, and focused on creating quality places, not just building units. 現代倫敦計劃以「優質增長」的理念指導所有發展——即社會包容、環境可持續，並專注於創造優質場所，而不僅僅是建造單位。
