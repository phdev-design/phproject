The Clear Choice in HVAC: R32 vs. R410A

The era of R410A refrigerant is ending. Driven by global regulations and superior performance, R32 is the definitive successor. This analysis breaks down why your next HVAC system should run on R32.

‚ú® Compounded Climate Benefits

R32's environmental advantage is more than just its lower GWP. Its superior properties create a multiplier effect, reducing the total climate impact by approximately 75%.

Less is More: Reduced System Charge

R32 is more efficient at transferring heat, so systems require less of it to achieve the same cooling effect. This reduces the potential impact from leaks and lowers material usage.

R32 systems typically require 20-30% less refrigerant charge by weight than an equivalent R410A system.

Smarter Power: Higher Energy Efficiency

Since 95-98% of an AC's lifetime emissions come from the electricity it uses, efficiency is paramount. R32 systems consume less power, reducing indirect emissions and saving you money.

On average, R32 systems are 5-10% more energy-efficient, leading to direct savings on your electricity bill.
